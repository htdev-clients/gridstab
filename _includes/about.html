Key Expertise Areas:

MTDC Grids: Operation and control of Multi-Terminal DC grids for efficient power transmission

EMT Simulations: Detailed electromagnetic transient analysis for power system dynamics

Low-Inertia Systems: Stability analysis and control of grids with high renewable penetration
